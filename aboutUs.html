About Us

Our Story

Sayeishaa Mep & Arch Solution is an end-to-end sustainability solutions provider, with core areas of focus being Green Building Projects, EIA Projects, Energy Audit Projects, Management System Projects, Testing & Commissioning Projects. As sustainability gets increasing focus among business leaders, we have tailored solutions covering different dimensions of sustainability to accelerate initiatives and fill capability gaps.

Mission

We are an architecture, interior designer partnership group in Bhubaneswar & India, We are best in residential and office design. We design as per your need and then you will enjoy the beautiful interiors. We will provide you one of the best guides also for your home and office. If you want to renovate your own apartment in Bhubaneswar? Or want to give your new design and timeless interiors? Sayeishaa Mep & Arch Solution, a professional in Bhubaneswar, will make your dream a success. We are doing Interior as well as exterior design.

Vision

As a Sustainability Solutions Provider… create tomorrow’s world, today with the least negative impact on the environment. We define and propagate values and practices for our partners in business and social communities to follow to ensure the protection of the environment, mitigation of the negative effects of development and urbanization, and reversal of at least some of the damages of the past. At the core of our business are the passion and integrity of our employees, all of whom are devotedly committed to leaving behind a cleaner, healthier, and safer environment for our future generations to live and work in.
